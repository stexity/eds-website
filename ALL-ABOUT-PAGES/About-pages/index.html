Our team of Engineers & Associates comprises of highly qualied and professional in the eld of Civil, Mechanical, Electrical with vast experience, locally and Internationally. EDS International is actively involved in Master Planning & Infrastructure development, multi-story building Designs and Construction, Rehabilitation and Strengthening of Existing structures, Precast concrete solutions for speedy construction, Industrial and Commercial Projects, Petro-chemical Complex, Chemical, Textile, Polyester, Food, Pharmaceutical and Personal Care Industries, Telecommunication Towers, Oil and Gas sector Well Sites Development, Mall Construction Local and Abroad.
